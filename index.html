Fundamentals

What is a GPU?

The Graphics Processing Unit: the engine behind every beautiful frame.

A GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) is a specialised processor designed to handle thousands of parallel computations simultaneously — making it indispensable for anything visual.

Think of your PC like a person braving winter:

🧥
CPU = Your body — handles thinking and decisions.
GPU = Your coat — handles the heavy lifting of visual work.
A better GPU means smoother, richer graphics performance.

Gaming

Renders complex 3D scenes at high frame rates for immersive gameplay.

AI & ML

Powers deep learning and neural networks with massive parallel compute.

Video Editing

Accelerates encoding, colour grading, and real-time 4K/8K preview.

3D & VR

Drives real-time rendering for architecture, product design, and VR.
